🏖️ Untouched Beaches

Experience pristine white-sand beaches, colorful coral gardens, and crystal-clear waters that remain largely undiscovered by mainstream tourism.

🌿 Unspoiled Nature

Dive into turquoise lagoon coves, trek through lush mangrove channels, and return to an island where time slows to a natural rhythm.

⛵ Easy Exploration

Navigate the island's 6 barangays by tricycle, join a bangka island-hopping tour, or hire a local guide who knows every sandbar and reef.

Why Patnanungan Belongs on Your Philippine Bucket List

From pristine coastlines to deep-rooted cultural heritage, experience an island where time slows down and adventure begins around every corner.
