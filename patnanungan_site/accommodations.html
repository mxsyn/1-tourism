Where to Stay

Accommodations

Simple, sincere, and right by the sea — Patnanungan's stays put you close to island life.

Patnanungan's accommodation scene is intimate and locally owned. Expect clean, comfortable lodgings that emphasize hospitality over luxury — authentic island experiences where hosts often become guides and friends.

🏖️

Beach Resorts

From ₱800–₱2,500/night · Cottages and fan rooms available

Several small beachfront resorts operate along White Beach and nearby shorelines. These typically offer nipa-roofed cottages with direct beach access, basic amenities, and meal service featuring fresh daily catch. Most can arrange island hopping and fishing trips for guests.

🏖️ Beach Access🍽️ Meals⛵ Boat Rental🛖 Cottages
🏠

Guesthouses

From ₱500–₱1,200/night · Private rooms with shared facilities

Locally run guesthouses in the poblacion and near the port offer budget-friendly rooms with shared bathrooms. These are practical options for travelers arriving late and departing early, with hosts who have deep knowledge of local schedules and transport.

🚪 Private Rooms🚿 Shared Bath🍳 Meals on Request⚓ Port Access
🤝

Homestays

From ₱300–₱800/night · Room in a local family home

The most immersive option — staying with a local family connects you directly to Patnanungan's daily life. You'll share meals, hear stories about the island's history, and get insider knowledge on the best spots that no tourist guide lists. Coordinate through the municipal hall or local tourism desk.

🍲 Home-cooked Meals🌺 Cultural Immersion🗺️ Local Knowledge

📋 Booking Information

  • Advance coordination is strongly recommended especially during peak season (Dec–Feb)
  • Contact the Patnanungan Municipal Tourism Office for updated accommodation lists
  • Cash only — no card payments or online booking systems currently on the island
  • Inform your host of your expected arrival time so they can meet you at the port
