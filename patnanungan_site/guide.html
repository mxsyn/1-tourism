Getting There & Around

Travel Guide

Reaching Patnanungan requires a bit of planning — but every step of the journey becomes part of the story.

How to Get There

  • From Manila to Lucena CityTake a bus from Cubao (EDSA), Buendia, or Pasay bound for Lucena. Travel time is approximately 3–4 hours. Bus lines: JAM Transit, Lucena Grand Terminal-bound coaches.
  • From Lucena to Real, QuezonFrom Lucena Grand Terminal, take a van or jeepney to Real, Quezon. This takes about 1.5–2 hours through the Quezon countryside.
  • Ferry from Real PortFrom the Real port, take a ferry to Patnanungan. The trip takes approximately 3–4 hours. Ferries depart early morning — check current schedules with the Real municipal port.
  • Local Sea TransportOnce on the island, smaller bangka (outrigger boats) serve as the main transport between barangays and nearby islets. Arrange rentals through your accommodation or local boatmen at the port.
  • Land Transport on the IslandTricycles and habal-habal (motorcycle taxis) connect the main poblacion to the different barangays. Fares are typically negotiated directly with drivers.

Best Time to Visit

November to May is the recommended travel window. Sea conditions are calmer and the weather drier, making ferry crossings more comfortable and beach activities more enjoyable.

December to February offers the coolest temperatures and clearest water visibility — ideal for snorkeling and underwater photography. This is peak season for island visitors.

June to October falls under the habagat (southwest monsoon) season. Expect rougher seas and possible ferry disruptions. Check weather forecasts closely before travel.

Suggested 2-Day Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrival & Beach Exploration

  • Morning Depart Manila, travel to Real, Quezon
  • Midday Ferry departs from Real Port
  • Afternoon Arrive Patnanungan, check in to accommodation
  • Late PM Short walk to White Beach, afternoon swim
  • Sunset Head to Compra Beach for sunset viewing
  • Evening Dinner of fresh seafood at local carinderia

Day 2 — Island Hopping & Departure

  • Dawn Sunrise viewing at White Beach
  • Morning Island hopping by bangka — Pulang Lupa + outer islets
  • Midday Snorkeling at reef + beachside packed lunch
  • Afternoon Return to poblacion, browse local market
  • Late PM Ferry back to Real, Quezon
  • Evening Overnight in Real or continue to Manila

⚠️ Safety & Preparation Tips

  • Always check ferry schedules in advance — they can change during bad weather
  • Bring sufficient cash — ATMs are not available on the island
  • Pack medicine, sunscreen, and insect repellent
  • Wear a life jacket on all boat trips
  • Check weather forecasts 2–3 days before travel
  • Download offline maps before leaving the mainland
