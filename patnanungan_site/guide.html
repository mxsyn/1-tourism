Getting There & Around

Travel Guide

Reaching Patnanungan requires a bit of planning, but every step of the journey becomes part of the story.

Transportation

How to Get There

Step 01
Manila to Lucena City
Bus from Cubao, Buendia, or Pasay via JAM Transit. Approx. 3–4 hours.
Step 02
Lucena to Real, Quezon
Van or jeepney from Lucena Grand Terminal. Approx. 1.5–2 hours.
Step 03
Ferry from Real Port
Morning ferry to Patnanungan island. Approx. 3–4 hours crossing.
Step 04
Local Sea Transport
Bangka boats between barangays and nearby islets.
Step 05
On the Island
Tricycles and habal-habal connect poblacion to barangays.
When to Go

Best Time to Visit

NOV – MAY
Dry Season

Recommended travel window. Calmer seas and drier weather make ferry crossings comfortable and beaches enjoyable.

Dec – Feb
Peak Season

Coolest temps and clearest water, ideal for snorkeling and underwater photography. Best conditions of the year.

Jun – Oct
Habagat Season

Southwest monsoon season. Rougher seas and possible ferry disruptions. Check forecasts closely before travel.

⚠ Safety & Preparation Tips
  • Check ferry schedules in advance as they can change during bad weather
  • Bring sufficient cash. ATMs are not available on the island
  • Pack medicine, sunscreen, and insect repellent
  • Wear a life jacket on all boat trips
  • Check weather forecasts 2–3 days before travel
  • Download offline maps before leaving the mainland
